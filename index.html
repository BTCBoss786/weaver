About UsWhat We Are

Weaver Garment Co is a young enterprise that manufactures good quality garments for the export and domestic sector. We are committed to keep the client at competitive edge by consistently providing superior value and competitive pricing.
With a focused view to attain the maximum level of customer satisfaction, we strive hard to deliver the best of our products and services to our customers. Through our sincere and consistent efforts, we have been able to clinch large number of clients from both domestic and international marketplace. Please don't hesitate to ask for more about our products.

Our Vision: “To emerge as a large manufacturing destination for retailers looking at sourcing value products”.
Exceeding Customer Expectation: Cater to client’s requirements with utmost importance and execution that safeguards their interests.
Quality Assurance: Producing quality garments forms a core value of our management practices. We believe in providing a Better working condition to our work force before delivering the highest quality service to our customers.
